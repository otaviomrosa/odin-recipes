Mapo Tofu

Description

Mapo Tofu is a famous Chinese dish composed mainly of tofu, meat and fermented bean curd.

This dish has recently become very popular worldwide for its peculiar umami-filled taste. My roommate introduced me to this dish and I can't stop eating it due to its simplicity and great taste.

Ingredients

  • Tofu (1lb)
  • Fermented bean curd
  • Ground chicken (0.5lb)
  • Sesame oil
  • Soy sauce
  • Sichuan peppercorn
  • Green onion

Steps

  1. Add a medium amount of sesame oil and around 20 sichuan peppercorns to a large frying pan.
  2. When the sesame oil is hot and the peppercorns are bubbling, add ground chicken and stir until chicken is almost fully cooked.
  3. Add two table spoons of fermented bean curd to the chicken and stir until the chicken is covered evenly with the bean curd.
  4. When the meat is cooked to your liking, add cubed tofu and stir until everything is evenly covered with the bean curd.
  5. When the tofu reaches your preferred consistency, turn off the stove and add the green onions. Stir and serve with your carbs of choice.
